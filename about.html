About Us

Berry & Cream is an evolution of Seoulful Sweets, reimagined as a modern neighborhood dessert and drink brand that focuses on comfort, creativity, and everyday indulgence.

Originally inspired by Korean desserts and beverages, the brand has grown beyond a single cultural identity to embrace a more universal, playful, and lifestyle-driven concept. Berry & Cream introduces a wider range of offerings—from fruit-forward drinks and matcha creations to soft pastries and signature sweets—designed to fit seamlessly into daily routines. With a focus on affordability, aesthetic experience, and grab-and-go convenience, Berry & Cream positions itself as a go-to spot for students, young professionals, and families looking for a small moment of joy in their day.

Mission

To create joyful, comforting treats that turn everyday moments into something a little more special—through thoughtfully crafted drinks, desserts, and experiences that are accessible to everyone.All menu items are subject to availability. Berry & Cream reserves the right to change, discontinue, or modify products without prior notice.

Vision

To become a beloved neighborhood dessert brand known for its playful identity, consistent quality, and ability to bring comfort and delight into people’s daily lives—one drink, one dessert, and one moment at a time.
